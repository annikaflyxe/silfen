PIPPI BAG

DKK 499

Pippi is the smaller version of our beloved Bibbi crossbody bag. For this season we have updated PIPPI with adjustable strings on the side, so you can decide the size of your bag. It comes with a classic shoulder strap and inside you will also find a nylon webbing strap, that can be attached on the outside, for a more comfortable crossbody strap. The bag has one main compartment with 2 smaller pockets inside. Features classic Silfen details as the SILFEN rubber logo on the strap. Strap: Adjustable shoulder strap and detachable crossbody strap.
